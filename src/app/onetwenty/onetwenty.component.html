Always operate on the principle of 120%

via the

20-40 40-20 Strategy



Allocate Your Income Intelligently

Whenever even 1 dollar comes to you it's time to manage money. Perform these actions:

  1. 20% => Invest
    • Determine what 20% of the new income is. Take that amount from existing storage
      and put it into a cash flow deal that generates a 20% ROI.
  2. 40% => Storage
    • Determine what 40% of the new income is. Put that amount into your savings account.
  3. 40% => Taxes
    • Determine what 40% of the new income is. Put that amount into a holy account.
  4. 20% => Lifestyle
    • Determine what 20% of the new income is. Put that amount into a checking account.

You can apply this model to many different scales (e.g., Annually, Monthly, Bi-Weekly)
